Examine Her Initiative

The Examine Her Initiative is focused on broad women's health needs: from community health sensitization and preventive screenings (such as breast cancer screening) to reproductive and maternal support. We place special emphasis on ensuring adolescent girls have access to dignified period care and education in underserved areas.

Activities include community health drives, screening and referral services, school and community-based sensitization on menstrual and reproductive health, mother-and-child support programs, and partnerships with local health providers to improve access and continuity of care.

We also connect women and girls to support networks, provide practical resources, and advocate for policies that enhance health equity across communities.

Happy Smile Donation

The Happy Smile Donation initiative embodies our commitment to community support and social responsibility. We focus on providing direct assistance to underprivileged women and children who need it most.

Our donation drives include essential items like clothing, school supplies, hygiene products, and food packages. We also organize special events during holidays and back-to-school seasons to ensure that families have what they need to thrive.

Beyond material support, Happy Smile Donation creates opportunities for community connection and empowerment. We partner with local organizations, schools, and healthcare centers to identify families in need and deliver support with dignity and respect.

Her Tech Rising

Her Tech Rising is our flagship program designed to bridge the gender gap in technology. We believe that women deserve equal representation and opportunities in the rapidly evolving tech industry.

Through mentorship programs, coding bootcamps, networking events, and career development workshops, we provide women with the tools, knowledge, and connections needed to thrive in technology careers. Our program covers everything from software development and data science to entrepreneurship and leadership in tech.

Since its launch, Her Tech Rising has supported over 500 women in their tech journey, with 85% of participants securing new roles or advancing their careers within six months of completing the program.
