Examine Her Initiative — Breast Cancer Screening (Dawa)

Brief: Breast Cancer Screening and Sensitization Exercise at Dawa, Ningo-Prampram Municipality.

Sistas Wing Union International successfully conducted a Breast Cancer Screening and Sensitization Exercise at Dawa as part of the “Examine Her” initiative.

Event date: Monday, 13 October 2025 — Exercise started at 9:00 AM and ended at 2:00 PM.

The “Examine Her” initiative aims to promote women’s health and early detection of breast cancer. The event began at 9:00 a.m. and included free screenings and health talks.

Health professionals educated participants on causes, symptoms, self-examinations, and treatment importance.

A total of 85 women were screened, with 13 cases referred for further medical attention. Survivor testimonies and emotional stories highlighted the importance of awareness.

SWUI reaffirmed its commitment to advocacy and regular screenings for women in rural areas.

The exercise ended with group photos, media coverage, and words of encouragement from partners.

Community Outreach — Empowering Through Service

Brief: Empowering communities through impactful initiatives, education, and sustainable support.

The Sistas Wing Union International continues its mission to make a difference through organized outreach programs focused on women, youth, and community growth.

Event date: Sunday, 5 October 2025 — Community support and engagement meeting.

Our team engaged residents with health education, youth mentorship, and small business training sessions aimed at promoting self-sufficiency.

Through partnerships and volunteering, we distributed essential supplies and provided hands-on assistance to families in need.

Over 100 participants benefited from skill-building sessions and educational talks on leadership, health, and social development.

Smiles and gratitude filled the day, reflecting the power of compassion and collective effort.

The event concluded with a shared meal, heartfelt speeches, and renewed commitment to community building.
