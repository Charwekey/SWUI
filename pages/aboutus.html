Sistas Wing Union International is a women-led, community-driven organization committed to restoring hope for girls, empowering women, and addressing essential needs in underserved communities.
Established in May 2025 by grassroots women leaders who refused to accept the status quo of poverty and vulnerability.
Our work aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), focusing on: Poverty reduction, Hunger eradication, Quality education, Gender equality, Access to clean water & sanitation, and Quality Healthcare.

What We Do:

Provide skills training programs to enhance employability and entrepreneurship
Offer donations and livelihood support to those in need
Foster community development and unity through service and leadership

Our Mission

Our mission is to restore hope to women and girls in underserved communities through unity, support, and meaningful initiatives that create lasting change in our communities.

Our Vision
